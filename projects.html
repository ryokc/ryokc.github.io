319 Mania!

This embedded systems project is based off of the popular rhythm games osu! mania, Dance Dance Revolution, or Guitar Hero. The device is built on a self designed pcb and houses a TM4C microcontroller alongside a DAC used for audio output, ADC, and four buttons. There are also two LEDs, an IR LED, and an IR receiver on the board that are not used for this project. The game was coded in C++ while most of the hardware interfacing was coded in C.

PCB layout

The goal is to hit the notes as they fall with the beat to the music. A score for accuracy and combo will be shown at the top of the screen. Additionally, there is a healthbar that will go down for missed notes and up for notes hit. The total score and counts of each note hit will be shown after the song is finished. The person will win if they make it to the end of the song without missing too many notes consecutively.

live gameplay

Skills: C, C++, ARM Cortex M4, DAC, ADC, LCD interfacing
